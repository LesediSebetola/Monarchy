About Us

Welcome to Monarchy Guest House, a cozy and comfortable bed and breakfast located in the heart of Johannesburg. Our guest house is situated just 2.1 km from Observatory Golf Club, 7.9 km from Parkview Golf Club, and 11 km from Gold Reef City Casino. We offer a 24-hour front desk and are located just 1.9 km from Johannesburg Stadium. Our friendly staff is always available to ensure that your stay is as comfortable and enjoyable as possible.

Our Services

Delicious Breakfast - Start your day off right with our delicious breakfast, served fresh every morning.

Garden - Our beautiful garden is the perfect place to relax and enjoy the fresh air.

Pool - Take a dip in our refreshing pool and cool off on a hot summer day.

Free Wifi - Stay connected with our complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi.

Daily Housekeeping - Our staff is dedicated to ensuring that your room is always clean and comfortable.

In-Room Dining Service - Enjoy a delicious meal in the comfort of your own room with our in-room dining service.

THINGS TO DO AROUND THE ARIA

Explore Johannesburg - There are many exciting things to do and see in Johannesburg, including cultural sites, markets, and more.

Observatory Golf Club - Located just 2.1 km from our guest house, the Observatory Golf Club is a great place to spend the day on the green.

Parkview Golf Club - This beautiful golf course is located just 7.9 km from our guest house and offers a challenging and enjoyable experience for golfers of all levels.

Gold Reef City Casino - Try your luck at the Gold Reef City Casino, located just 11 km from our guest house

Bird Watching - South Africa is home to a wide variety of beautiful and fascinating birds, making it the perfect destination for bird watchers.

orseback Riding - Explore the beautiful South African countryside on horseback with one of the many horseback riding tours available in the area.
